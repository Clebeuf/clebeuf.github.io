My name is Carlene Lebeuf, and I am an aspiring front end developer. In spring 2014, I graduated with distinction from the University of Victoria, Canada, with a combined Bachelor's of Science Degree in Computer Science and Psychology. Currently, I’m working on my master's degree in a research lab called the CHISEL Group (Computer-Human Interaction Software Engineering Lab), at the University of Victoria.

I love designing and implementing beautifully simple, user-centered interfaces. When I am not pushing pixels, I enjoy DIY projects, cooking, and gardening. Whenever possible, I love to travel and spend time in the natural beauty of the great outdoors.
